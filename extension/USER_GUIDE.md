## üêõ Troubleshooting

### Extension Not Working

**Symptoms:** No highlights, extension icon grayed out

**Solutions:**
1. Refresh the page (Cmd/Ctrl + R)
2. Check if signed in (click extension icon)
3. Verify domain is enabled (Settings tab)
4. Ensure you're on supported marketplace
5. Try disabling/re-enabling extension

### Highlights Not Showing

**Symptoms:** Watchlist has keywords but nothing highlighted

**Possible causes:**
- Highlights disabled in settings
- Wrong marketplace selected
- Keywords not matching exact text
- Page loaded before extension

**Solutions:**
1. Check Settings ‚Üí "Keyword Highlights" is ON
2. Verify watchlist has terms for current market
3. Reload page after signing in
4. Wait a moment after page load

### Tooltips Not Appearing

**Symptoms:** Hover doesn't show metrics

**Solutions:**
1. Check Settings ‚Üí "Tooltips" is ON
2. Hover longer (tooltip has slight delay)
3. Ensure keyword is highlighted
4. Check internet connection (metrics fetch online)

### Authentication Issues

**Symptoms:** "Not signed in" message

**Solutions:**
1. Click "Sign In" in extension popup
2. Complete auth flow in web app
3. Return to extension (should auto-update)
4. If stuck, try Sign Out then Sign In again

### Performance Issues

**Symptoms:** Page slow to load, browser lagging

**Solutions:**
1. Reduce watchlist size (<100 keywords per market)
2. Disable on resource-heavy pages
3. Clear extension data (Settings ‚Üí Clear Data)
4. Update to latest version
5. Report issue to support

## üìä Understanding Metrics

### Demand Index (0-100)

**What it measures:** Search volume and interest

- **0-20**: Very low demand (niche)
- **21-40**: Low demand
- **41-60**: Moderate demand (sweet spot)
- **61-80**: High demand
- **81-100**: Very high demand (competitive)

**Strategy:**
- Beginners: Target 40-70 range
- Experienced: Can compete at 70+
- Avoid <20 unless very niche strategy

### Competition Score (0-100)

**What it measures:** Market saturation

- **0-20**: Low competition (opportunity!)
- **21-40**: Moderate competition
- **41-60**: Medium-high competition
- **61-80**: High competition
- **81-100**: Very saturated (avoid)

**Strategy:**
- Look for low competition + moderate demand
- High demand + high competition = hard to rank
- Low demand + low competition = potential gem

### AI Opportunity Score (0-1)

**What it measures:** Our algorithm's recommendation

**How it's calculated:**
- Combines demand, competition, trend
- Factors in seasonality
- Adjusts for market dynamics
- Considers historical success

**Interpreting:**
- **0.7-1.0**: Strong opportunity
- **0.5-0.7**: Worth exploring
- **0.3-0.5**: Proceed with caution
- **0.0-0.3**: Likely not profitable

### Trend Direction

**What it measures:** Recent momentum

- **‚Üë Rising**: Growing interest (2-4 weeks)
- **‚Üì Falling**: Declining interest
- **‚Üí Stable**: Consistent search volume
- **? Unknown**: Insufficient data

**Strategy:**
- **Rising** + **Good Score**: Jump on it!
- **Stable** + **Low Competition**: Evergreen potential
- **Falling**: Avoid unless countertrend play
