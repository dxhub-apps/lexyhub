# LexyHub Help Center

Welcome to the LexyHub Help Center. This collection of guides is designed for merchants, growth managers, and analysts who rely on LexyHub to discover opportunities, optimize listings, and monitor cross-marketplace performance. Use this page as your launchpad into the rest of the public documentation.

## Product Overview
LexyHub is an AI-powered commerce intelligence workspace that helps you stay ahead of marketplace trends, understand customer intent, and launch data-backed campaigns. Explore the [Business Overview](./business-overview.md) to learn how LexyHub packages competitive intelligence, pricing visibility, and marketplace monitoring into one simplified platform.

## Getting Started
- **Sign in and explore the workspace:** The [User Workspace Guide](./user-documentation.md) walks through navigation, dashboards, and recommended workflows for daily operations.
- **Install the Chrome extension:** Follow the [Extension User Guide](./EXTENSION_USER_GUIDE.md) to capture on-page insights while browsing Etsy, Amazon, Pinterest, and more.
- **Launch Pinterest automation quickly:** The [Pinterest Quick Start](./PINTEREST_QUICK_START.md) and [Pinterest Setup Guide](./PINTEREST_SETUP.md) outline the configuration steps merchants need before scheduling their first syncs.

## Feature Highlights
- **Unified trend intelligence:** Monitor demand shifts, keyword performance, and high-intent signals across marketplaces from one dashboard.
- **Listing optimization workflows:** Use AI-assisted editing flows to refresh product titles, descriptions, and tags with confidence.
- **Marketplace extensions:** Capture competitive insights and keyword ideas in real time via the browser extension while you browse storefronts.
- **Performance analytics:** Stay on top of KPIs with ready-to-use dashboards that surface opportunities and alert you to anomalies before they impact revenue.

## Frequently Asked Questions
**Who is LexyHub for?**  
Multi-channel sellers, growth teams, and marketplace operators who need continuous visibility into search demand, pricing, and competitor moves.

**Do I need technical knowledge to onboard?**  
No. The setup guides walk you through the required steps in plain language, and LexyHub’s automation handles the rest once credentials are connected.

**Can I manage multiple storefronts?**  
Yes. Switch workspaces within the app header to isolate analytics and automations per storefront or brand.

**How does LexyHub support marketing campaigns?**  
LexyHub pairs audience insights with campaign-ready keyword lists, allowing you to launch targeted ads and content with confidence.

**Where do I find troubleshooting tips?**  
The [Pinterest Troubleshooting](./PINTEREST_TROUBLESHOOTING.md) guide covers common sync issues, and in-app notifications highlight anything that needs your attention.

## Need Help?
Still have questions? Reach out through your LexyHub success channel or use the in-app feedback button. For billing or account updates, contact your account representative directly so we can keep your storefronts running smoothly.
